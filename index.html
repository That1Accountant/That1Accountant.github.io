About InflataFun Parties

InflataFun Parties specializes in event and party management in the Mid Michigan area. We pride ourselves in providing the absolute best bounce houses, battle balls, sno cones, cotton candy, and popcorn in the industry.

The best part... WE bring the equipment. WE set it up. WE make the snacks. WE regulate safety on the bounce houses...

YOU have FUN!

We also pride ourselves in providing the cleanest and safest equipment in the industry. Each unit is cleaned and sanitized after every use to guarantee your safety and satisfaction. We always follow the strictest safety guidelines and we absolutely will not sacrifice your safety for business. EVER.
